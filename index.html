The Really Cuckoo Company

At Cuckoos we're a local community made up of

  • Singers
  • Dancers
  • Actors
  • Volunteers

Cuckoos is currently focusing on two different projects.

One being run by all the members under the age of eighteen. While the other being run by all the over eighteens.

These two projects are rather diverse and are nothing like eachother. This allows the younger members of Cuckoos to really express and perform what matters to them.

The over eighteens have been eager to put on a more risque show for a long time and this allows us to finally do that. We are putting on a show called Noises Off. A sex farce!

While the younger generation are bringing you their very own cabaret called Spotlight.
